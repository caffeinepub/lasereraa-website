# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Create a professional website for Lasereraa, an industrial laser cutting organization that offers metal, MDF, and acrylic cutting services plus corporate and customizable gift items.

**Planned changes:**
- Build homepage with hero section featuring Lasereraa brand and lasereraa.com domain
- Create services section highlighting laser cutting capabilities for metal, MDF, and acrylic
- Add products section showcasing corporate gifts and customizable gift items
- Implement sharp industrial design with metallic tones, clean lines, and technical typography
- Include contact section with company information and inquiry call-to-action

**User-visible outcome:** Users can view Lasereraa's professional industrial website with services, products, and contact information, featuring a precision-focused aesthetic that reflects the company's laser cutting expertise.
